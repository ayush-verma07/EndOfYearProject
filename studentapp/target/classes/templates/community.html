How it Works

You can join topic channels for any topic or create your own and assign topic tags to your question/topic channel. this will help people (especially verified users) locate your questions and answer them as fast and effectively as possible. you can also request to open a direct message with them, or any other user you encounter through community connections. if you are talking in two chats and don’t want to have to keep switching between them (especially if one is a direct message and the other is a topic channel) you can open both and display both. however, you can only do this with 2 at max.

To switch from direct messages to topic channels, use the arrows on the side of the box. as always, don’t forget to be careful online and practice proper cyber etiquette. Follow community guidelines and don’t purposely provide people with misinformation, AS PENALTIES CAN AND WILL BE ADMINISTERED. TREAT OTHERS HOW YOU WOULD LIKE TO BE TREATED.
